Be a Hero

You don't have to be a
front-liner to be a hero.

Here are some ways to be a Health Hero while fighting COVID-19 in your community!

  • Make masks from old fabric and donate them!
  • Donate food to your local food bank!
  • Find a pen pal who might want company!
  • Support local businesses!
  • When outside, stay six feet apart from people outside your household!
  • Send a card to a healthcare worker or first responder!
  • Help someone at home with a task!
  • Donate extra essentials!
  • Stay inside!
  • Don't overstock on food and essentials!
  • Participate in online volunteering!
  • Reach out to someone who might need help!
  • Call up your friends - they might be lonely!
  • Help out seniors with tasks like retrieving food!
  • Donate blood!
  • Donate medical supplies!
  • Support your essential workers!
